Choclate Cake

Ingredients

Cake bottom

  • 150g of butter
  • 250g of dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 125g sugar [1,5 dl]
  • teaspoon of salt
  • 90g flour[1,5 dl]

Ganache

  • 100g of butter
  • 150g of dark chocolate
  • cane sugar [1,5 dl]
  • 3 egg yolk
  • 150g cream [1,5 dl]
  • 60g honey [0,5 dl]

Steps

Cake bottom

  1. Turn on oven to 175 degress C.
  2. Melt butter in a pot and add the chocolate, when it is all melted put to the side
  3. Whisk the sugar, eggs and single egg yolk in a bowl
  4. When homogeneous add the butter chocolate mixture
  5. Sift in flour and mix
  6. Add the mixture to a cakepan and put into the oven for 13-15 minutes
